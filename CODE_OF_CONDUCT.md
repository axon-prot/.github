# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (Version 2.1)

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in
our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open,
welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our
community include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual
  attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or harassing language, or personal or political
  attacks
- The posting of non-consensual or private material
- The public or private harassment of others
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered harassment or
  discriminatory in nature

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may
be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers are obligated to respond to any incident. For
additional guidance, see the full `Contributor Covenant` source for the
process of dealing with incidents.
